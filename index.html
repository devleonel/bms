All you questions are important. Allow us to clear any doubts or concerns for your peace of mind.

Bariatric surgeries are a set of medical procedures in which the goal is to reduce the stomach’s size, so the portions to be eaten after that are naturally smaller, still satisfying, and rapidly absorbed by the body, resulting in a massive, general, and especially evident weight loss.

Although it implies dealing with the stomach and maybe the intestines directly (depending on the procedure you choose), there is no need to be alarmed as the process is done through laparoscopy, and the external incision is small, taking very little time in healing compared to other surgical invasive practices. Besides, our team of bariatric experts in Tijuana, Mexico, are board-certified and provide medical care at world-class facilities. They will assist you from beginning to end in this journey, so you rest assured knowing you are in the best hands.
